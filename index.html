About Me

Turning Data into Decisions

I am a 3rd-year BSc Data Science student at the Co-operative University of Kenya, Nairobi. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, applied statistics, and software engineering — focused on building systems that actually ship and deliver value.

From commodity risk modelling and SME credit scoring to AI-powered supply chain optimization, every project I build is cloneable, deployable, and production-oriented. I believe data science that never leaves a notebook doesn't solve problems.

I co-founded AIRA (Applied Intelligence & Research Associates) with a bold mission: leverage AI and analytics to build transformative solutions across healthcare, finance, and agriculture in Africa.
